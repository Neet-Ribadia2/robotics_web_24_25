Marketing


Our marketing division strategically designs themes centered around each robot each year. We help to enhance its identity through our curated exhibit and through cohesive thematic elements. Our team integrates creative concepts with technically aspects of construction to ensure a blend between innovation and storytelling. This approach helps to emphasize and showcase the robots purpose and its functionality in engagingly visual ways. Our marketing team works to elevate the robot's impact and broaden the appeal to not only our judges but to consumers and audience members.

Programming


Our programming division is dedicated to supporting the organization and all of its various functions. We are divided into two subdivisions: Robot Programming and Website Development. Robot Programming focuses on perfecting the performance of the robots to ensure ease of maneuverability for pilots. Website Development is solely responsible for maintaining and optimizing the organization's website to be as compact and efficient as possible.

Construction


The construction part of a robotics team involves robot construction, creation of a practice field, and the handling of collaboration. The whole team does the design, assembling, and testing to ensure everything works correctly and as it should be for competition requirements. Creating the field allows the team to simulate conditions very similar to those in a real competition for practice. The job is divided among the members according to their skills, each contributing to the mechanical, electrical, or programming aspect.
